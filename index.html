About Me

I'm a third-year undergraduate currently pursuing a Masters of Engineering in Aeronautical Engineering at Imperial College London. Aviation is inspiring to me as it has enabled global connectivity on a whole new level in the last century, and I'm fortunate to be in an environment where everybody around me are equally excited about the possibilities in the future in this discipline. I'm most interested in the computational aspects of the discipline, such as fluid dynamics and structural mechanics, but seeing everything come together on an aircraft is also exciting!

I'm an avid problem solver, and am always up for a challenge! I'm also a quick learner, especially if it involves me learning as I go, and I feel that I learn best through working through problems. I also enjoy tinkering and attempting to dissect everything that comes my way.

Outside of school, I spend most of my time playing board and card games of all sorts, especially contract bridge. I also enjoy a good book once in a while.
